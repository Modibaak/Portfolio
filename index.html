About Me

I'm a Full-stack Developer and Marketer

Hi there! I am a Marketing graduate from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology, where I completed my Advanced Diploma in Marketing in 2023. While my academic foundation is rooted in marketing principles and business strategy, I have recently developed a strong interest in technology, particularly web development, which I have taught myself using platforms such as GitHub and Visual Studio Code.

Since 2019, during my first year of university, I have been blogging on WordPress about topics related to branding, student life, and digital culture. This experience naturally led me into content creation on TikTok, where I now produce mock campaigns, test popular trends, and share relatable content focused on audience engagement and brand visibility.

In my spare time, I enjoy enrolling in online courses that help me grow both professionally and personally. I am especially interested in the following areas:

- Market Information and Analytics (Statistical Analysis, Quantitative and Qualitative Research) - Management and Finance for Marketers - Branding and Communication - UX and UI Design - Front-end Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) - Copywriting - Other subjects that enhance my understanding of marketing and technology

I am passionate about combining creativity with strategy to build meaningful digital experiences and drive impactful marketing campaigns.
